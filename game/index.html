Description

Resident Evil 2 takes the original 1998 game and it goes into uncharted territory for the story, gameplay and its supporting characters as well. This remake is more a re-imagining of the original than anything else, because the gameplay is totally different but the atmosphere is still there; and this one's got a more grounded approach. This one feels like a horror movie being acted out in real time, and its immersion is just right for 2019 gamers.
